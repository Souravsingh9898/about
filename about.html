About Me

Hi, I’m Sourav Singh, born on 4th September 2002 in Punjab. I lived there until 2014, after which my family and I shifted to Delhi. My roots are in Bihar, my primary education was in Punjab, and I’m currently pursuing my higher studies in Delhi.

I have 1 year and 7 months of experience in Customer Support, where I built strong communication skills and the ability to handle challenges effectively. Along with that, I’ve completed an advanced Excel course and gained good knowledge of other Microsoft tools.

Creativity has always been close to me — I’ve worked as an actor for a YouTube channel, once managed my own channel, and picked up skills in writing, editing, and content creation along the way.

A couple of personal milestones I’m proud of: in 2023, I bought myself a gaming laptop from my own earnings, and recently I purchased a bike, both of which hold special value for me.

Outside of work and studies, I’m passionate about fitness, gyming, and bike riding. I also love creating videos, not as a profession, but as something that satisfies me creatively.

I enjoy spending time alone, reflecting, and exploring different experiences in life. My goal is to learn from every field I step into and grow as an individual.
